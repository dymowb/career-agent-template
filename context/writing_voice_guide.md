# Writing Voice Guide

> The single source of truth for voice. The drafter writes to it and the voice judge enforces
> it, so put your tone and your banned words/phrases HERE rather than expecting them to be
> hardcoded. Describe how your writing should sound so generated letters read like *you*, not AI.

## Voice in one line
e.g. "Warm but precise. Direct. A practitioner who's read the JD, not a marketer."

## Do
- Open with something specific to the role/company.
- One concrete achievement per paragraph; pair action with outcome.
- Vary sentence and paragraph length.
- Plain, confident language.

## Don't
- No uniform paragraph blocks (a tell of AI generation).
- No claims not backed by the source of truth.
- No overselling or hedging.

## Banned words and phrases
The voice judge flags any of these (and the drafter avoids them). List the clichés YOU hate:
- "passionate", "results-driven", "enthusiastic", "fast-paced environment"
- "I am excited to apply", "what excites me most"
- "leverage", "innovative solutions", "bridge the gap", "I thrive in environments"

## Sample (optional but powerful)
Paste 1–2 paragraphs you actually wrote and like. The drafter will echo this rhythm.
