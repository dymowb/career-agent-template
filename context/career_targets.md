# Career Targets

> Used by the scorer to decide whether a role is worth pursuing. Be concrete — the scorer
> compares each job against these criteria.

## Target roles / titles
- e.g. Senior Engineering Manager, Director of Engineering, Staff Engineer

## Target companies
List or describe the kind of companies you want (named companies, stage, industry).
Note: company *filtering* for discovery lives in `agents/discovery.py` (TARGET_COMPANIES)
and `config.py` (COMPANY_SKIP / COMPANY_BLOCK_UNTIL).

## Level / seniority
The bands you'll accept (and ones you won't, e.g. "no first-line-only roles").

## Compensation
Base / total floor. The hard salary filter is `MIN_BASE_COMP` in `config.py`.

## Location / remote
Remote, hybrid, specific cities, visa/relocation constraints.

## Deal-breakers
Anything that should auto-disqualify a role.
